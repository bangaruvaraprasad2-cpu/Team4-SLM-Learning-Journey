Kakinada Institute of Engineering and Technology (KIET) was established in 2001.

KIET is located in Korangi, East Godavari District, Andhra Pradesh.

The college offers B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA, and Diploma courses.

The college is approved by AICTE.

The college campus has a library, laboratories, hostel facilities, and sports facilities.

The admission process is based on AP EAMCET for undergraduate programs.

The college provides placement assistance and training programs.

The principal of the college is responsible for academic administration.

The college has Computer Science, Electronics, Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, and Artificial Intelligence departments.
